About Us

CricStarter is a website that provides free and basic guidelines, techniques, and tips to young children who are interested in learning the sport of cricket. The website is designed to help children learn the fundamentals of cricket and improve their skills. It features cricket analysis by renowned experts such as Aakash Chopra, Shane Warne, Kevin Pieterson, and Joe Root, whose tips and tricks are sure to help young learners groom their cricketing skills.
The sole purpose of creating this platform is to provide free cricketing tips to young learners who are passionate about the sport. CricStarter is grateful to its partners such as ICC, Shikana, and Wisedn cricket boards for providing some of their tutorials for learning cricket.
The platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to children from all backgrounds, even those in remote areas who may not have access to professional coaching or training. With CricStarter, young learners can access the guidance and support they need to improve their skills and develop a love for the sport of cricket.
